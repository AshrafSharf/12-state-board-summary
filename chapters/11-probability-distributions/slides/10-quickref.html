Finding Mean & Variance

TypeMean $\mu = E(X)$Variance $\sigma^2 = V(X)$
General Discrete$\sum x\,f(x)$$\sum x^2 f(x) - \mu^2$
General Continuous$\int x\,f(x)\,dx$$\int x^2 f(x)\,dx - \mu^2$
Bernoulli$(p)$$p$$pq$
Binomial$(n,p)$$np$$npq$

Given mean & variance (Binomial): $q = \frac{\sigma^2}{\mu}$, then $p = 1 - q$, then $n = \frac{\mu}{p}$.
