Is it about scalar/vector products?
YES

↓
Volume or coplanarity of vectors?
YES

NO ↓

Nested cross product $\vec{a} \times (\vec{b} \times \vec{c})$?
YES
BAC-CAB Rule

All Formulas at a Glance

$$\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = a_1b_1 + a_2b_2 + a_3b_3 = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\cos\theta$$
$$\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b} = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\cos\theta$$
$$\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{vmatrix} \hat{i} & \hat{j} & \hat{k} \\ a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\ b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \end{vmatrix}, \quad |\vec{a} \times \vec{b}| = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\sin\theta$$
$$|\vec{a} \times \vec{b}| = |\vec{a}||\vec{b}|\sin\theta$$
$$[\vec{a},\vec{b},\vec{c}] = \begin{vmatrix} a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\ b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \\ c_1 & c_2 & c_3 \end{vmatrix} \qquad \vec{a} \times (\vec{b} \times \vec{c}) = (\vec{a} \cdot \vec{c})\vec{b} - (\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b})\vec{c}$$
$$\begin{gathered} [\vec{a},\vec{b},\vec{c}] = \begin{vmatrix} a_1 & a_2 & a_3 \\ b_1 & b_2 & b_3 \\ c_1 & c_2 & c_3 \end{vmatrix} \\[8pt] \vec{a}\!\times\!(\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{c}) = (\vec{a}\!\cdot\!\vec{c})\vec{b} - (\vec{a}\!\cdot\!\vec{b})\vec{c} \end{gathered}$$
$$\underbrace{\text{Line: } \vec{r} = \vec{a} + t\vec{b}}_{\text{Parametric}} \quad \longleftrightarrow \quad \underbrace{\frac{x-x_1}{b_1} = \frac{y-y_1}{b_2} = \frac{z-z_1}{b_3}}_{\text{Cartesian}}$$
$$\begin{gathered} \vec{r} = \vec{a} + t\vec{b} \\[4pt] \frac{x\!-\!x_1}{b_1} = \frac{y\!-\!y_1}{b_2} = \frac{z\!-\!z_1}{b_3} \end{gathered}$$
$$\underbrace{\cos\theta = \frac{|\vec{b}\!\cdot\!\vec{d}|}{|\vec{b}||\vec{d}|}}_{\text{Angle between lines}} \qquad \underbrace{\delta_{\text{skew}} = \frac{|(\vec{c}\!-\!\vec{a})\!\cdot\!(\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{d})|}{|\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{d}|}}_{\text{Shortest distance (skew)}} \qquad \underbrace{d_{\parallel} = \frac{|(\vec{c}\!-\!\vec{a})\!\times\!\vec{b}|}{|\vec{b}|}}_{\text{Distance (parallel)}}$$
$$\begin{gathered} \cos\theta = \frac{|\vec{b}\!\cdot\!\vec{d}|}{|\vec{b}||\vec{d}|} \\[8pt] \delta_{\text{skew}} = \frac{|(\vec{c}\!-\!\vec{a})\!\cdot\!(\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{d})|}{|\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{d}|} \\[8pt] d_{\parallel} = \frac{|(\vec{c}\!-\!\vec{a})\!\times\!\vec{b}|}{|\vec{b}|} \end{gathered}$$
$$\underbrace{\vec{r}\!\cdot\!\vec{n} = p}_{\text{Plane}} \qquad \underbrace{\theta_{\text{planes}} = \cos^{-1}\!\left(\frac{|\vec{n}_1\!\cdot\!\vec{n}_2|}{|\vec{n}_1||\vec{n}_2|}\right)}_{\text{Plane-plane angle}} \qquad \underbrace{\theta_{\text{line-plane}} = \sin^{-1}\!\left(\frac{|\vec{b}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}|}{|\vec{b}||\vec{n}|}\right)}_{\text{Line-plane angle}}$$
$$\begin{gathered} \vec{r}\!\cdot\!\vec{n} = p \\[8pt] \theta_{\text{planes}} = \cos^{-1}\!\left(\frac{|\vec{n}_1\!\cdot\!\vec{n}_2|}{|\vec{n}_1||\vec{n}_2|}\right) \\[8pt] \theta_{\text{line-plane}} = \sin^{-1}\!\left(\frac{|\vec{b}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}|}{|\vec{b}||\vec{n}|}\right) \end{gathered}$$
$$\underbrace{\delta = \frac{|ax_1\!+\!by_1\!+\!cz_1\!+\!d|}{\sqrt{a^2\!+\!b^2\!+\!c^2}}}_{\text{Point-to-plane}} \qquad \underbrace{\delta = \frac{|d_1-d_2|}{\sqrt{a^2\!+\!b^2\!+\!c^2}}}_{\text{Parallel planes}} \qquad \underbrace{\vec{v} = \vec{u}+\frac{2(p-\vec{u}\!\cdot\!\vec{n})}{|\vec{n}|^2}\vec{n}}_{\text{Image of point}}$$
$$\begin{gathered} \delta_{\text{pt-plane}} = \frac{|ax_1\!+\!by_1\!+\!cz_1\!+\!d|}{\sqrt{a^2\!+\!b^2\!+\!c^2}} \\[8pt] \delta_{\parallel\text{planes}} = \frac{|d_1-d_2|}{\sqrt{a^2\!+\!b^2\!+\!c^2}} \\[8pt] \vec{v} = \vec{u}+\frac{2(p\!-\!\vec{u}\!\cdot\!\vec{n})}{|\vec{n}|^2}\vec{n} \end{gathered}$$
$$\underbrace{(\vec{c}\!-\!\vec{a})\!\cdot\!(\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{d})=0}_{\text{Coplanarity}} \qquad \underbrace{(\vec{r}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}_1\!-\!d_1)+\lambda(\vec{r}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}_2\!-\!d_2)=0}_{\text{Family of planes}} \qquad \underbrace{\vec{u}=\vec{a}+\frac{p-\vec{a}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}}{\vec{b}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}}\vec{b}}_{\text{Line-plane intersection}}$$
$$\begin{gathered} (\vec{c}\!-\!\vec{a})\!\cdot\!(\vec{b}\!\times\!\vec{d})=0 \;\text{(coplanar)} \\[8pt] (\vec{r}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}_1\!-\!d_1)+\lambda(\vec{r}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}_2\!-\!d_2)=0 \\[8pt] \vec{u}=\vec{a}+\frac{p-\vec{a}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}}{\vec{b}\!\cdot\!\vec{n}}\vec{b} \end{gathered}$$
